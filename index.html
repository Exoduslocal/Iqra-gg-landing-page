The Importance of "Iqra"

The word "Iqra" means "read" or "recite" in Arabic. It is the first command revealed to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), emphasizing the importance of knowledge, learning, and education in Islam. "Iqra" signifies the beginning of a journey towards understanding and enlightenment, making it a cornerstone of Islamic faith and practice.
