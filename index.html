The Meaning and Importance of "Iqra"

The word "Iqra" means "read" or "recite" in Arabic. It is the first word revealed to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) in the Holy Quran, emphasizing the importance of knowledge, learning, and education in Islam. The command "Iqra" signifies the beginning of a journey towards understanding and enlightenment, making it a cornerstone of Islamic faith and practice.
